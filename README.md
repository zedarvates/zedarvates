I am a self-taught systems architect with a background in industrial sciences
and a creative practice spanning 3D, game engines, and generative media. I design
systems where AI agents, local inference, and real-time tools work together as
coherent, reproducible pipelines.

I care about software that remains understandable, runs close to the user, and
turns ambitious research ideas into practical tools.

## What I build

- **Agent systems** — orchestration, memory, skill graphs, evaluation, and
  multi-model workflows.
- **Local-first AI** — offline inference, voice interfaces, GPU and edge
  runtimes, and privacy-conscious automation.
- **Creative engines** — generative storytelling, procedural 3D, VR rendering,
  spatial media, and artist-facing tools.
- **Systems software** — Rust and Python backends, performance engineering,
  robotics experiments, and secure device communication.
