### About Me

Trained in industrial sciences and techniques, I built most of my engineering skills outside traditional paths. I'm an artist at heart, and that shows in how I build: I treat AI agents, 3D engines, and embedded systems as creative mediums, not just tools.

My work spans four areas that keep showing up across my repos:

- **AI agent architectures** — orchestration frameworks, memory, skill bundles, and self-evaluating pipelines for autonomous agents.
- **Local-first / offline AI** — voice assistants, TTS, and inference stacks that run on your own hardware, no cloud dependency.
- **Creative & generative pipelines** — multimedia generation (image, video, story, audio) and procedural 3D/VR tooling.
- **Robotics & secure systems** — air-gapped communication, drone/robot control, and low-level performance work (Rust, CUDA).

I learn by shipping. Most of these projects started as "can this even work" experiments and turned into working systems.
